<h1>Leja's Trinkets</h1>

Features: 
---
    Rings: 
    Equippable rings that gives specific status effects when worn.

    The material of the ring affects both durability and status effect level:
    - Bronze: Low Durability, Low Level Effect
    - Iron: High Durability, Medium Level Effect
    - Gold: Medium Durability, High Level Effect
    - Netherite: Highest Durability, Highest Level Effect

    The Gemstone affects what Status effect You'll get on your ring:

    Quiver:
    Equippable in the Cape slot. Can store different amount of arrows, depending on the quality:
    - Leather Quiver: 
        2 Stacks of arrows. 
        Craftable using 8 Leather in a circle.
        Allows for quick-switching between arrows when worn.

    - Hardened Quiver 
        3 Stacks of arrows.
        Crafted by upgrading a Leather Quiver using 8 Armadillo Scutes.
        Allows for quick-switching between arrows when worn.

    - Dragon Quiver 
        4 Stacks of arrows.
        Crafted by upgrading a Hardened Quiver using a Dragon Head and 2 Shulker Shells
        Allows for quick-switching between arrows when worn & another effect...
